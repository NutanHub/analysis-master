Frequently Asked Questions

How does MSBTE PaperHub analyze question papers?
MSBTE PaperHub uses advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to extract text from uploaded papers using OCR technology, then analyzes question patterns, frequencies, and topics across multiple years to predict weightage.
What file formats are supported?
We support PDF files and image formats including JPG, JPEG, and PNG. Each file can be up to 50MB in size, and you can upload multiple files simultaneously.
Is MSBTE PaperHub free to use?
Yes! MSBTE PaperHub offers free analysis for all users. You can upload and analyze question papers without creating an account. Premium features and detailed reports may require registration.
How accurate are the predictions?
Our AI models achieve over 95% accuracy in pattern recognition and weightage prediction based on historical data analysis across thousands of question papers.
Do you support handwritten question papers?
Yes, our advanced OCR technology can process handwritten text, though accuracy is highest with clear, legible handwriting. Typed or printed papers generally yield the best results.
